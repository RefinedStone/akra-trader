Quant Admin UI Research

2026-05-04

Lazyweb MCP was confirmed healthy on 2026-05-05 and used for the quant-focused iteration. Browse screenshot tooling is still not available, so references are documented from Lazyweb metadata and vision descriptions.

Lazyweb References

  • Yahoo Finance compare: dense stock metric comparison and market sidebars.
  • AlgoTest backtest: strategy result metrics, report actions, and context panels.
  • Harmoney: risk scenario tables for VaR/CVaR and what-if simulation panels.
  • Yahoo Finance crypto: sortable coin table with market cap, volume, changes, and sparklines.
  • AlgoTest simulator and Webull paper trading: paper execution, P&L, charts, and portfolio views.

Direction

  • Favor command-center information density over marketing-style hero composition.
  • Present Akra Trader as a stock/crypto quant admin product.
  • Use Korean-first labels for backtesting, market data, paper trading, and guarded live execution.
  • Put status, data source, and metric context near the top for fast operator orientation.
  • Use calmer tokens with purposeful accent colors for operational states.
  • Improve form, table, card, and button consistency globally.
